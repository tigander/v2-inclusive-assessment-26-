Reflection questions

                                             How do I evaluate the knowledge and skills that are directly
Consider the following
                                             related to learning goals and expectations?
questions in your own
context.
                                             How do I ensure assessments are accessible, flexible, ongoing,
                                             and used to inform teaching and learning?



                                             Do I provide multiple means for students to express their
                                             thinking? How could I strenthen this?



                                             Can I co-construct more assessments with students whenever
                                             possible?



                                             Are all students offered the tools and approaches they need to

                                             version, additional time)?
